About the Artist

Daggy is an international award-winning tattoo artist specializing in realism tattoos

Her work focuses on creating highly detailed, lifelike pieces in both black and grey and vibrant color.

Known for her precision, smooth shading, and ability to capture incredible depth, Daggy transforms ideas and images into tattoos that feel alive on the skin

From powerful portraits to striking realism pieces, every tattoo she creates is approached with patience, artistry, and attention to detail.

With over a decade of experience and countless tattoos inked, Daggy has built a reputation for delivering stunning, custom tattoos that exceed client expectations.
